Park & Ride Information

AAD has developed a park-and-ride system to make parking easier. Zwartkop Airbase and Air Force 68 Air School have been identified as official parking areas, while a third parking area will be located inside the AFB Waterkloof.

Cars will not be allowed to park next to the road to avoid obscuring traffic.
